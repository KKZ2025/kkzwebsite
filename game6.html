Character Guessing Game

Answerer

Choose a character and prepare to answer questions

Questioner

Ask questions to eliminate characters and find the answer

Game Rules

1

2-3 players per group. One player is the Answerer, the others are Questioners.

2

The Answerer secretly selects a character without telling others.

3

Questioners take turns asking yes/no questions using the "V+着" structure.

4

Example questions: "他穿着裙子吗？" (Is he wearing a skirt?) or "他站着吗？" (Is he standing?)

5

The Answerer answers "是" (yes) or "不是" (no) based on their selected character.

6

Questioners eliminate characters based on the answers and try to guess the Answerer's character.
